Biography

I’m Jiaqi, a second-year grad student at XJTLU, nestled in the charming city of Soochow, China. Prior to entering graduate study, I dabbled in the software industry, which gave me a solid foundation. These days, my curiosity has led me to the intriguing fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW).

I’m a new contributor to the Apache open source community, and I’m also just starting to explore the field of online ethnography. In the former case, I continue to write code to understand how computers work. In the latter case, I am interested in how the web - computer connects people, and I continuously observe and explore this, which has gradually become my way of unraveling the world.
